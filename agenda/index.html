Agenda

Cloud, Maker, Community: Source and Data, Startup are 4 main themes of Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015.

RSVP is required. HKOSCon 2015 is free of charge. To cover some of conference expenses, individual donations are available on registration.

Free Registration »

Keynotes, talks and workshops will be included at Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015. Sessions are 40 minutes each in general, keynotes are 45 minutes and workshops are 90-150 minutes. Some accepted topics are announced in March, more topics will be coming soon.

Last updated on 27 May 2015. Sessions are subject to changes without notice.
