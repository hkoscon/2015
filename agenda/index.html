Agenda

Cloud, Maker, Community: Source and Data, Startup are 4 main themes of Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015.

RSVP is required. HKOSCon 2015 is free of charge. To cover some of conference expenses, individual donations are available on registration.

Free Registration »

This is preliminary agenda of Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015. Keynotes and Talks (including SITCON Talks) are 45-mins and 40-mins each with 10-15 minutes break between sessions.

Some accepted topics are announced in March, more topics will be coming soon.

Sessions are conducted in English (with English slides) except marked with *2 to *5.
