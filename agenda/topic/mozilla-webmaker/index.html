Mozilla Webmaker

Speaker: Irvin Chen (Taiwan)

Community: MozTW

Language: Mandarin (with English Slides)

Category: Open Source

Tag: Workshop Mozilla

Beginners

About Speaker

MozTW community liaison, 9 years of Mozillian, contributing to Mozilla on web design & community building. My day job is F2E engineer at womany.net

About the Topic

(Please bring your laptop to the workshop if you can)

Webmaker (webmaker.org) is Mozilla's educational project, including 4 tools and hundreds of teaching guides for people to learn/teach how to build on the web/app, and to increase people's web literacy ability.

This workshop will guide through all of those tools, introducing resources of Webmaker, let participant making small web page, video and app, and help them to understanding what is Webmaker's goal.
