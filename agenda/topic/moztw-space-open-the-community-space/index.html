摩茲工寮 MozTW Space: Open The Community Space

Speaker: Orin Chen (Taiwan)

Community: Mozilla Taiwan Community

Language: Mandarin (with English Slides)

Tag: community

Beginners

About Speaker

Orin Chen is Internet, Mozilla, Open Web, and Open Source lover. He contributes to Mozilla by host many events, planning, translate and sharing his Mozilla stories in university. He is also a co-maintainer of Mozilla Community Space Taipei.

About the Topic

Introduce about Mozilla Community Space Taipei, how we support the open web and open source by sharing our community space. What kind of problem we found, how we fix it.
