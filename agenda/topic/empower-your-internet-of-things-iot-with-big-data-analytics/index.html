Empower your Internet-of-Things (IoT) with Big Data Analytics

Speaker: Ying-Kit Wong (Hong Kong)

Silver Sponsor: IBM China/Hong Kong Limited

Language: English (with English Slides)

Tag: internet of things big data

Beginners

About Speaker

Ying-Kit Wong is Senior Technical Specialist, IBM Cloud at IBM China/Hong Kong Limited.

About the Topic

As much as 90 percent of all data generated by devices such as smartphones, tablets, connected vehicles and appliances is never analyzed or acted on. Explore how you can derive deep business insight from the Internet of Things (IoT)-- an integrated fabric of devices, data, connections, processes, and people –with IBM.

New IoT services as part of IBM Bluemix will enable developers to easily bridge the IoT data with Big Data Analytics capability. Developers will be able to enrich existing business applications – such as enterprise asset management, facilities management, and software engineering design tools – by infusing more real-time data and embedded analytics to further automate and optimize mission-critical IoT processes.
