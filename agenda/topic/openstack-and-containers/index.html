Openstack and containers

Speaker: Gustavo Panizzo (China)

Language: English (with English Slides)

Category: Cloud

Tag: meteorjs

Intermediate

About Speaker

I'm and OpenStack operator since Grizzly release, LXC user since 2011, Linux Guy since 2001.

I enjoy working with networks, specially if they are virtual. From Argentina living in China :)

About the Topic

I will talk about how do i deploy OpenStack inside operating system containers, why i do it and when does it make sense to do it. Also i will talk about how can we use OpenStack to deploy operating system containers, what challenges does it present and what are the advantages of doing it.
