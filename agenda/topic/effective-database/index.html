Workshop: Effective database

Speaker: Triton Ho (Hong Kong)

Language: Cantonese (With English Slides)

Category: Startup

Tag: Database

Beginners

About Speaker

An evangelist of PostgreSQL and open source databases. Triton Ho is actively involved in multiple opensource community(especially HKLUG and backend-tw), to help others building system on top of opensource solution.

About the Topic

Instead of focusing on usage of a specific database products, this workshop will provide the general knowledge on database. In this workshop, we will revise basic concept of Relational Database, showing why RDBMS is more suitable to rapid development for small to medium sized system. noSQL will also discussed in this workshop.
