Meeting Global Open Source Community

Hong Kong Open Source Conference on 26-27 June.

Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015 (HKOSCon) is the International Tech Conference hosted in Hong Kong, driven by the Open Source Community. HKOSCon is aimed to connect the global Open Source technology and communities.

30 overseas speakers and 20 local speakers will deliver 50 sessions for 2-day 6-track 3rd-year Open Source conference with 20 exhibition booths by vendors and communities. HKOSCon 2015 is free of charge.

Hong Kong Open Source Conference 2015

Date: 26-27 June 2015 (Friday - Saturday)
Time: 10:00am - 6:30pm
Venue: Charles K Kao Auditorium and Conference Halls, Hong Kong Science Park, Shatin.
Web Site: http://2015.opensource.hk

Online Registration: http://hkosc2015.eventbrite.com

Vendors and Commercial Open Source Users - Amazon Web Services, Red Hat, Oracle, IBM, Paypal, New Media Group, Microsoft, Gandi, OurSky and 43 Global will have keynotes, talks and booths to demonstrate their contribution and use of Open Source technology.

Global Open Source Communities - Linux Kernel, GNOME, VideoLAN, Mozilla, MySQL, MariaDB will visit in town again this year to deliver talks and workshops. Speakers from Data Science and Makers will also participate in the conference, Open Source Community and Makers Community will demonstrate their works at exhibition halls. You can also bring your Open Source projects to our development sprints to collaborate and discuss with your old and new friends at HKOSCon too.

See you at HKOSCon 2015 !
